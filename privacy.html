CMM is committed to your privacy. In order to maintain your privacy, we are only able to discuss patient matters directly with the member who was treated by the medical providers. The member may provide written consent to CMM to speak with an authorized representative. It is the nature of the ministry to share in the costs of other members' medical needs and thus requires communication with third-party providers.
